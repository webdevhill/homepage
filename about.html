A bit about me.


When I was younger computers were not prevalent in my daily life or my education. I never learned how to use them. I found computers to be  somewhat intimidating. I enrolled in the Electronics Engineering program at Pitt Community College and started learning some things. In our first lab, we took apart a Dell tower and rebuilt it from the motherboard to the power supply. Suddenly computers seemed more accessible to me and no longer  such a mystery.  We programmed some Arduinos, which introduced me to such a unique open-source culture. And then we were given a four-week block  that provided the basics of HTML and CSS. That's all it took. I wanted to be a web developer and learn programming.
